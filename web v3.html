Introduction

We are going to develop an app for fraud detection and prevention. If the user receives messages with suspicious patterns, a notification will pop up.

Fraud Statistics

Therefore, our app will focus on detecting potential fraud occurring on social media.

Objective

We have an AI model that drives the operation of the app with various algorithms. Our goal is to build an AI model for fraud detection and prevention with real-time notification. The system can identify suspicious messages and alert users about potential fraud by leveraging Natural Language Processing(NLP) and Behavior Pattern Analysis. This topic is important because fraud has become a significant issue, especially with the increasing use of mobile phones for financial transactions and sensitive information access. The proposed AI model can enhance public awareness and empower users to protect themselves from fraudsters.

Technologies Used

  • Natural Language Processing(NLP)

    • It is an area of AI that focuses on enabling computers to understand and process human language. It involves developing algorithms and models that can analyze and interpret text or speech, allowing machines to perform tasks such as language translation, sentiment analysis, and information extraction. NLP utilizes techniques from machine learning and linguistics to make sense of language, enabling applications like chatbots, voice assistants, and text analytics. It has broad applications across industries and has the potential to enhance human-computer interaction and automate language-related tasks.

  • Behavior Pattern Analysis

    • It is a methodology used to identify and analyze recurring patterns of behavior in various contexts, such as human behavior, consumer behavior, or system behavior. It involves collecting and examining data to uncover regularities, trends, and anomalies in behavioral patterns. By identifying these patterns, insights can be gained into underlying factors, motivations, and potential outcomes. Behavior Pattern Analysis often employs statistical techniques, data mining, and machine learning algorithms to extract meaningful information from large datasets. It has applications in fields like cybersecurity, fraud detection, and detect unusual or suspicious activities.
